Kartãu di Biblioteka / Library Card

Kartãu grátis pa rezidentis! / Free card for all residents!

Kum é pa konsigi un kartãu? / How to Get a Card

  1. Pasa na biblioteka durante orariu di funsionamentu. / Visit the library during open hours.
  2. Meste un ID ku foto (pa ex. kartãu di identifikason, passaporte, ou kartãu di motorista). / Bring a photo ID (e.g., state ID, passport, or driver’s license).
  3. Meste un komprovativu di morada (pa ex. konta di luz, áwa, ou kartãu di banku ku morada). / Bring proof of address (e.g., utility bill, lease, or bank statement with address).
  4. Preenxi formuláriu di inskriçon. / Fill out the registration form.
  5. Recebi kartãu di biblioteka na ora. / Receive your library card immediately.

Kosa bu pode fasi ku kartãu? / What Can You Do with a Card?

  • Empresta livrus, DVDs, i CD di música. / Borrow books, DVDs, and music CDs.
  • Uza komputador i impresora na biblioteka. / Use library computers and printers.
  • Acesa WiFi grátis. / Access free WiFi.
  • Acesa rekursu online (e-books, audiolivrus, i mais). / Access online resources (e-books, audiobooks, and more).
  • Partisipa na eventus i programa. / Participate in events and programs.
  • Reserva sala di estudu. / Reserve study rooms.

Kum é pa Reserva / How to Reserve

  1. Meste kartãu di biblioteka válidu. / Have a valid library card.
  2. Fasi reserva na balcão di informason ou ligá pa (555) 123-4567. / Make a reservation at the information desk or call (555) 123-4567.
  3. Reserva pode fasi 1 simana na frenti. / Reservations can be made up to 1 week in advance.
  4. Limitason di 2 óra pa reserva. / 2-hour limit per reservation.
  5. Si bu ta 15 minutu atrasadu, reserva pode sí anulá. / If you are 15 minutes late, your reservation may be canceled.

Servisu di Passaporti / Passport Services

Biblioteka ta oferese servisu di aplikason pa passaporti. / The library offers passport application services.

Kos ki é oferesedu? / What's Offered

  • Aplikason pa passaporte Merkanu (livru i kartãu) / U.S. passport applications (book and card)
  • Servisu di fotografia pa passaporte / Passport photo service
  • Prosesamentu regular i aseleradu / Regular and expedited processing
  • Renovason di passaporte / Passport renewal

Kum é pa apliká? / How to Apply

  1. Fasi un markason 401-725-3714 x212. / Make an appointment by calling 401-725-3714 x212.
  2. Meste dokumentu di sidadaia (sertifikadu di naskimentu ou naturalizason). / Bring citizenship evidence (birth certificate or naturalization certificate).
  3. Meste un ID ku foto válidu (kartãu di motorista, ID di stadu, ou passaporte anterior). / Bring a valid photo ID (driver’s license, state ID, or previous passport).
  4. Meste un kópia di ID (frente i tras). / Bring a photocopy of your ID (front and back).
  5. Preenxi formuláriu DS-11 na biblioteka. / Fill out form DS-11 at the library.
  6. Pagu taksa di aplikason i taksa di aseitamentu ku txekis separadu. / Pay the application fee and acceptance fee with separate checks.

Taksas / Fees

Livru (adultu): $130 / Book (adult): $130
Kartãu (adultu): $30 / Card (adult): $30
Livru (kriansa): $100 / Book (child): $100
Txeka di aseitamentu: $35 / Acceptance fee: $35

Pagu ten ki ser fasi ku txekis separadu pa "U.S. Department of State" i pa "Biblioteka Públika". Neka aseita dinheru ou kartãu di kréditu. / Payment must be made with separate checks to "U.S. Department of State" and "Public Library". No cash or credit cards.

Pamodi é midjor fasi na biblioteka? / Why Apply at the Library?

  • Markason konvenienti, sen fila lonji. / Convenient appointments, no long lines.
  • Staff ta papiá Kriolu i Inglês pa ajuda bu. / Staff speaks Kriolu and English to assist you.
  • Fotografia disponível na lokál. / On‑site photo service available.
  • Apoiu pa verifiká dokumentu antis di envia. / Help reviewing your documents before submission.

Nota: Prosesamentu normal pode dura 8-11 simana. Aseleradu (2-3 simana) disponível ku taksa adisional. / Note: Routine processing takes 8-11 weeks. Expedited (2-3 weeks) available for an additional fee.
